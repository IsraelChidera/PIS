Crèche

Working in partnership with busy and career parents (mothers especially), our ultra-modern Crèche facility, caters for babies between 3 -18 months. Our educated Caregivers provide structured, individualized, caring relationship to each baby. We are open every day except public holidays and weekends. Our operational time is 7.30am - 5.00pm.

Preschool (Playgroup/Nursery)

Welcome to our World! Making the right Pre-school choice, guarantees good childcare and excellent childhood foundation that lasts a lifetime! At Platinum, we have created an environment where every child feels special and loved, this gives them the confidence to express themselves freely. Equal opportunity is given to every child between 3months and 5years to transition from one milestone of development to another. Ours is an ambience of love and fun where practical skills of Early Childhood Education are enhanced. In addition, it embraces other areas of child development which encompass social, emotional, spiritual, moral and physical development. This is otherwise referred to as a holistic education. A world where children explore under the careful guidance of thoughtful Practitioners to build healthy relationships as resilient learners who leave the Preschool with self-confidence, love for learning and other skills to enjoy a happy productive Primary school life. Each pupil is allowed to learn at his/her own pace

Primary

The Primary Curriculum is a careful blend of the British National Curriculum and the strength of the Nigerian Curriculum. Our teaching and learning methodology in the classroom incorporates the 4Cs of 21st century learning skills (Creativity, Communication, Collaboration and Critical thinking).This ensures that knowledge and skills are built systematically and consistently to support transition from one key stage to another. Outdoor learning is also an integral part of our curriculum; hence pupils embark on field trips to further concretize learning in the classroom. Our final products therefore, are well equipped and ready to conquer the world as leaders.

Our Curricular activities are divided into various categories
.

Core Curriculum: Numeracy, Literacy, Science, Social Science, Religious Education, Design and Technology, P.S.H.E, Arts, Music, ICT, French .

.

Co-Curricular Activities: Swimming, Chess and Scrabble, UC Mas, Coding and Robot

.

Extra-Curricular Activities (electives): Karate, Cub Scout, Football, Instrumental Music and Balle

.

Club Activities: Editor’s Ink, Literary and Debate, Home Makers and Spelling and Scrabble.

Studying in PLATINUM is not all work and no play. Fun is guaranteed, in the true spirit of exploration with discipline! That is why physical, emotional, creative and intellectual development of children is the hallmark of Platinum Education. We believe in raising holistic children. At PLATINUM, pupils are given equal opportunity to register and participate in any activities of their choice (for electives) to discover their innate talent, nurture to fruition and showcase them to the world.
